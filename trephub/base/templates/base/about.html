Our Story

trepHub grew out of a community of coders, hackers, and startup founders to support building a vibrant tech startup ecosystem on the Space Coast. It began as an experiment, a place that provided fun workspace to collaborate on projects, make forward progress, and build something great. We set up free Internet access, lots of whiteboards, open workspaces, and gave teams an creative environment to push their projects ahead with opportunities to interact with other engineers and designers, attend events, and provide a professional face to those early companies and teams. In addition to supporting those startups in the trepHub offices, we are reaching out to the community. Through CHF, our affiliated tech meet up community, we host quarterly hackathons, a monthly speaker series, and regular lunch time and after work networking events. You can find out more about these events and see info about past events at our Events page along the top of the screen. trepHub is a non-profit corporation whose charter is to support the tech community and technology startups through Education, Community, and Infrastructure support.
